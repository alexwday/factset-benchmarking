# Category

Filters the list of FF_* metrics by major category -   * **INCOME_STATEMENT** = Income Statement line items, such as Sales, Gross Profit, Net Income.   * **BALANCE_SHEET** = Balance Sheet line items, such as Assets, Liabilities, and Shareholders Equity.   * **CASH_FLOW** = Cash Flow Statement line items, such as Financing activities, Operation, and Per Share.   * **RATIOS** = Pre-calculated Ratios, including Financial, Growth Rates, Profitability, Liquidity, Size, and Valuation.   * **FINANCIAL_SERVICES** = Financial Statement Items modified for Financial Services companies.   * **INDUSTRY_METRICS** = Industry Specific Line Items or Modifications. View subcategory for list of Industries.   * **PENSION_AND_POSTRETIREMENT** = Accumulated Pension Benefit Obligations and related data.   * **MARKET_DATA** = General Market Data, such as Shares Outstanding. *Note - /factset-prices/prices/ endpoints may be better suited for pricing related market data.*   * **MISCELLANEOUS** = Corporation Data, Financial Records details, Indicators.   * **DATES** = Relevant Dates
